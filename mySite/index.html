Hello,

I am Félix Almonte

As a software developer my experience mainly consists of C++, some javascript and C#. I've also dabbled with commercial game engines such as Gamemaker Studio 1 & 2, which uses a language similar to C++. I was able to release an indie game, which was a retro-platformer called Occultus Command. Unfortunately, as I was trying to make the game compatible with older PCs, it ended up suffering from low-quality graphics and compressed audio. I am currently making a sequel, simply as a hobby, but this time I'm not limiting the game to a certain size. I am using high-quality art assets and uncompressed wave audio for sfx.

As far as personal hobbies, I enjoy various grappling sports such as Judo, Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, and freestyle wrestling. I am not able to practice or compete as much as I'd like due to prior injuries, but I try to stay active as best I can. I also enjoy graphic design, mainly in the style of 90s to early 2000s US comic books, or in the style of gritty manga such as Berserk or Biomega. I've done some commission work consisting of promotional art, webcomic covers and graphic novel art.

I also enjoy travelling to various states and sightseeing. Last year I travelled to a few amusement parks, a water park, a renaissance fair, some beaches and went hiking to several local parks.
